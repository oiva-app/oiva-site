This page will compare Oiva with existing product categories and explain the design space that shaped the project.

## Standalone Autonomous Agents

Some products approach incident response as a broad autonomous SRE workflow. The final version will contrast those systems with Oiva's narrower investigation-reporting scope.

## Observability Platform Assistants

Observability-native tools can provide rich context inside a platform, but they may be tied to one vendor's data model and user interface.

## Incident Management Tools

Incident platforms help teams coordinate response. Oiva focuses earlier in the flow by enriching alerts before responders spend time manually collecting context.
