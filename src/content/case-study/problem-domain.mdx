This page will describe the operational problem Oiva addresses: teams already receive alerts, but alerts often leave the next investigation step unclear.

## Alert Context

Production alerts are useful only when responders can quickly understand what changed, what is affected, and what evidence supports the next action.

## Investigation Overhead

The final copy will explain the repeated work involved in jumping between telemetry, repositories, dashboards, and communication tools during early incident response.

## Useful Automation

Oiva's target is not replacing engineers. It is reducing the cost of gathering and organizing initial evidence.
